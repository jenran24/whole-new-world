Who is Scout?

Scout is a German Shepherd-mix who loves many things - cardboard, whipped cream, chasing cats, backscratches, and walks. His two-legged parents have had him for 4 years and are obsessed with taking him on hikes and walks. Yet, Scout is unique - his walks are never the same. Here, we'll document Scout's unique walking habits.

Where does Scout like to go?

  • The beach
  • The park
  • Local trails
  • Mountains

What does Scout do?

  • Smell EVERYTHING
  • Look for friends
  • Get the zoomies

What does Scout dislike?

  • Water falling from the sky
  • Not stopping for smells
  • Bees/Flies
  • The neighborhood cat, Mowgli
